Welcome to Virtual Beans Café!

At Virtual Beans, we believe that great coffee starts with the purest, most natural beans. Our mission is to bring you an exceptional coffee experience that honors the craft, sustainability, and flavor of high-quality coffee. We are passionate about sourcing only the finest beans from around the world, ensuring that every cup reflects our commitment to purity and excellence.


Our Coffee Philosophy

Coffee is a journey that begins long before it reaches your cup. We are dedicated to offering a selection of pure, natural beans that have been grown and harvested with care. Our approach focuses on sustainable farming practices that respect both the environment and the farmers who cultivate these incredible beans. By choosing to work with direct trade partners, we guarantee that our beans are ethically sourced, supporting communities while promoting organic and eco-friendly practices.


The Power of Pure Beans

The difference between ordinary coffee and extraordinary coffee lies in the beans. Our selection features a variety of single-origin coffees, each with its unique flavor profile that showcases the terroir of its origin. From the bright, fruity notes of Ethiopian Yirgacheffe to the rich, chocolatey undertones of Colombian Supremo, each cup tells a story of its land, climate, and the care taken in its cultivation.

We firmly believe that coffee should be as close to its natural state as possible. That’s why we avoid artificial flavors and additives, focusing on the inherent qualities of our beans. Our commitment to purity means you can enjoy the authentic taste of coffee without compromise.


Brewing Techniques

At Brewed Awakening, we utilize various brewing methods that highlight the unique characteristics of our pure beans. Each method allows for different flavor nuances to shine through, ensuring that every cup is an experience.

Espresso: Our espresso is a concentrated shot made from finely-ground natural beans, creating a bold foundation for many of our specialty drinks. We take pride in our precise extraction techniques to maximize flavor.

Pour Over: This meticulous method involves pouring hot water over coffee grounds in a filter, allowing for optimal extraction. The result is a clean, aromatic cup that accentuates the distinct flavors of our single-origin beans.

French Press: For a full-bodied experience, our French press coffee allows the natural oils and flavors of the beans to infuse into every sip. This method celebrates the richness of pure coffee without any filtration interference.

Cold Brew: Our cold brew is steeped for hours using coarsely ground beans, resulting in a smooth, naturally sweet coffee that is perfect for warm days. It’s a refreshing way to enjoy the purity of our high-quality beans.


Our Community Connection

That’s why we frequently host events that allow you to meet local roasters, learn about coffee brewing techniques, and understand the journey of your favorite beans from farm to cup.
